🔑 Backup & recovery — you can't get locked out

Everything stored here is a convenience copy: every key can be re-issued from its provider (Anthropic, Google, Razorpay, Meta) or re-pasted here, and the Railway env vars stay a live fallback. So a lost encryption key never loses the app or your data — worst case, you re-enter a key once.

To change the encryption key: just update SETTINGS_ENCRYPTION_KEY in Railway. Decryption also tries JWT_SECRET, so anything keyed to it still opens; a value saved under the old key simply shows "not set" — re-paste it and it re-encrypts with the new one.
